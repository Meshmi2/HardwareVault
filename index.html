Our Mission

HardwareVault was created to preserve the history of personal computing by collecting reliable technical information, benchmark data, and original photographs of classic computer hardware. Our goal is to build a free, searchable resource for enthusiasts, collectors, historians, technicians, and future generations interested in the evolution of computer technology.
